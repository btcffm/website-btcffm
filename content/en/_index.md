The Frankfurt Bitcoin Meetup continues the legacy of the first “original” Frankfurt Bitcoin Meetup, which was founded in 2013. Back then, it took place in a cozy coworking space called [Zentrale] in Frankfurt-Bornheim.

[Zentrale]: https://die-zentrale-ffm.de/

Fortunately, after a hiatus, some Bitcoin veterans revived the meetup. Since then, we’ve been meeting **every third Thursday of the month.**

From September through April, we usually look for an indoor venue where enthusiastic folks occasionally prepare exciting Bitcoin-related talks.

In the summer, we meet outdoors—in parks, at barbecue spots, or in beer gardens. Suggestions for cool locations are always welcome!

The meetup is run by a volunteer organizing team and financially supported by the community. We’re grateful for donations. These help us cover the costs of venues and, if necessary, drinks. Everything is transparent and all for BTC adoption!

You can contribute [here](https://geyser.fund/project/frankfurterbitcoinmeetup).

For the latest information on meeting spots, follow us on our social media channels.

Plebs together strong!
